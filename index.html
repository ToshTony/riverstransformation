Rivers of Transformation Counselling Center

Our Center was founded with a mission to provide holistic and compassionate counselling services to those navigating emotional, psychological, and spiritual challenges. Inspired by the belief that healing flows like a river—steady, cleansing, and transformative—we offer professional support that meets you where you are.

  • Compassion

    We listen without judgment.

  • Empowerment

    We equip you to thrive beyond challenges.

We are a safe space for individuals, couples, and families seeking support through life’s most difficult challenges. We are committed to walking with you on your journey toward healing, restoration, and personal growth. Whether you are facing mental health struggles, relationship breakdowns, or life crises, we are here to help you find peace and purpose.

Services

Marital & Family Counselling

Rebuild trust, communication, and connection in your relationships. Whether you're dealing with conflict, infidelity, or parenting challenges, we provide tools to help your family heal and grow.

Mental Health & Emotional Well-being

We support individuals struggling with: Stress and burnout Depression and anxiety Suicidal thoughts and emotional overwhelm

Adolescent and Youth Counselling

Support for teens facing peer pressure, identity issues, substance experimentation, academic stress, and emotional confusion. We also guide parents in better understanding their adolescents.

Grief, Loss & Pain Counselling

Whether you're coping with death, divorce, miscarriage, or other significant loss, our debriefing sessions provide a safe space to express, process, and begin healing.

Trauma, Crisis & Accidents

We provide trauma-informed care for survivors of accidents, violence, or other life-altering events. Learn grounding techniques, regain control, and find your path forward.

Substance Abuse & Addiction Recovery

We work with individuals battling drug and alcohol abuse through counselling, relapse prevention strategies, and support for long-term recovery.

Economic Hardship & Joblessness

Counselling for those navigating unemployment, financial stress, and feelings of hopelessness. Gain support, practical coping strategies, and goal-setting tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have Questions? We're Here to Help.

We understand that seeking counselling can feel like a big step, and it's natural to have questions. Below are answers to some of the most common questions we receive about our services, confidentiality, and how to get started. If you need more information, don’t hesitate to contact us directly—we’re here to support you every step of the way.

Do I need a referral to start counselling?

No, you can contact us directly.

How many sessions will I need?

It varies, but most clients benefit from 6–12 sessions.

Are your services faith-based?

Yes, but we are inclusive of all beliefs.

Do you offer online/virtual sessions?

Yes, available by appointment.
